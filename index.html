About This Project

This blog documents my individual project for EN8905. I have chosen SDG 7 – Clean Energy as my focus SDG, supported by SDG 13 (Climate Action) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities). The central question I investigate is:

"Should Bahrain and MENA transition from fossil-fuel power generation to utility-scale Solar PV — and what are the technical, economic, ethical, and social trade-offs?"

I am an electrical engineering student at Bahrain Polytechnic and work in the ICS department at SABIC, giving me direct insight into industrial energy consumption in the Gulf.
