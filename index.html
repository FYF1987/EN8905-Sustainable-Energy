About This Project

This blog documents my individual project for EN8905. I have chosen SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy as my focus SDG, supported by SDG 13 (Climate Action). The central question I investigate is:

“Should Bahrain accelerate its transition from a natural-gas-dominated power grid to utility-scale Solar PV — and what are the technical, economic, ethical, and social trade-offs of doing so?”

I am an electrical engineering student at Bahrain Polytechnic. This blog was substantially revised in July 2026 to check every technical claim against official sources — IRENA, the IEA, IPCC lifecycle-emissions literature, and Bahrain’s Electricity and Water Authority (EWA) — and to correct figures that earlier drafts had gotten wrong (most notably, the status of the Al Dur Solar project — see Blog 2).
